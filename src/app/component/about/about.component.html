ABOUT

Jared G. Kraemer is a Florida-born photographer who specializes in natural landscapes, urban architecture, beautiful portraits and stunning weddings. He fell in love with his first camera at 12 years old and has been honing his skills ever since.

Over a decade after picking up his first camera, Jared has become an amazing source for professional photos of sweeping landscapes, magnificent architecture and lively events. Jared is comfortable shooting in all environments from the busy streets of Paris to crowded yachts for wedding ceremonies to the top of Half Dome in Yosemite National Park.

Jared G. Kraemer Photography is currently based out of Tampa, Florida, but Jared enjoys traveling the world to capture new, incredible experiences.
